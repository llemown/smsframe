SCHOOL SEAL

The school seal is compromised of the following symbols: torch, hands, laurel, leaves, book, and blue mountain.

The torch symbolizes light sennt by the God to give learners an enlightenment, comfort, and strength. It unites us with one another by the divine love He pours forth in our hearts (Home 5:5)

The hands represent the caring touch and humble faith of St. Margaret Mary of Alacoque who possesses unconditional love for humanity and who promotes devotion to the Sacred heart of Jesus. It shows school's capacity to hone learners creative and critical thinking skills.

The laurel leaves symbolize academic excellence and success.

The book emblemalizes knowledge and wisdom that will serve as an armour to every learner's battle in attaining quality education.

The blue mountain symbolizes the zenith of hardwork, hope, courageous love and genuineness toward leraners' conduct and discipline and it shows the school initiative to impart the love for Mother Earth who provodes life and shelter to humankind.

The school seal signifies the core values that aims to achieve for learners.
